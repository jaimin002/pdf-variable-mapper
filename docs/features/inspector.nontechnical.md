## Inspector Panel (Non‑Technical Overview)

### What this feature does
- Shows **details about the field** you clicked on in the PDF.
- Lets you **change its key, label, and type**, or delete it if you no longer need it.
- Provides a button to **export all your variable definitions as JSON**.

### How you use it
- Click a field on the PDF.
- In the inspector on the right:
  - Edit the **Key** (internal name, e.g. `customerName`).
  - Edit the **Label** (what humans see).
  - Adjust the **Type** if you chose the wrong one.
- You can also see which page the field is on and its approximate position.

### Why it matters
- The inspector is where you **fine‑tune your template**, without needing to touch code.
- It ensures your exported data structure uses clear, consistent keys and labels.
